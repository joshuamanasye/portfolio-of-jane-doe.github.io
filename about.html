Jane Doe.

No name. No record. No cause of death they could agree on.

They found me in the dirt. Unmarked, undisturbed, and smiling. They should have left me there.

Origin

I was buried alive with a curse sewn into my skin. Every mark on my body was put there by men who feared what they could not name.

I did not die. I endured. There is a difference.

The Craft

I bend what should not bend. I know the words written before language had a name. I have turned the storm, stilled the heart, and walked out of rooms that were locked from the inside.

Call it a blessing.

Find Me.

I have always been in the room.

You just had to open the drawer.
